About Lesson Plan.Ai

Welcome to Lesson PlanAi! This website is dedicated to support teachers in creating engaging and effective lesson plans aligned with the Western Australian curriculum. This platform offers a powerful AI-driven lesson plan generator, powered by Google's Gemini AI, designed to streamline your planning process and inspire innovative teaching.

We understand the demands placed on educators, and our goal is to provide a valuable resource that saves you time and effort. Our AI takes your input and generates comprehensive lesson plans, incorporating the key elements of the Western Australian curriculum framework.

In addition to the AI-powered lesson plan generator, we also provide a curated collection of online resources. This library of materials is designed to complement your lesson plans and offer a wealth of supplementary materials for your daily teaching.

This project is a collaborative effort with the teachers of Westbridge International School of Phnom Penh. Their expertise and insights have been instrumental in shaping the development of this platform, ensuring its relevance and practicality for educators. We are grateful for their partnership and ongoing support.
