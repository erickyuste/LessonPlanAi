Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


Q: What is the AI-powered lesson plan generator?

A: Our AI-powered lesson plan generator uses Google's Gemini AI to create lesson plans based on the Western Australian curriculum. You provide input such as subject, year level, and topic, and the AI generates a detailed lesson plan based on that.


Q: How accurate are the lesson plans generated by the AI?

A: The AI is trained on the Western Australian curriculum and designed to create plans that align with its framework. However, it's essential to review and adapt the generated plans to ensure they meet the specific needs of your students and classroom context. The AI is a tool to assist you, not replace your professional judgment.


Q: What kind of online resources are available?

A: We provide a curated collection of online resources, including websites, articles, videos, and other materials relevant to the Western Australian curriculum. These resources are designed to supplement your lesson plans and provide additional support for your teaching.


Q: Who is behind this website?

A: This website is a collaborative project developed in partnership with the teachers of Westbridge International School of Phnom Penh. Their expertise has been invaluable in creating this resource for educators.


Q: Is there any copyright infringement related to the content on this website?

A: No. This website features logos and content from Westbridge International School of Phnom Penh with their permission. These elements are used to acknowledge their collaboration and contribution to the project. There is no copyright infringement intended. All other content is either original or used with appropriate licensing.


Q: How can I contribute to the website or provide feedback?

A: We welcome your feedback and suggestions! Please contact us through the Web Form with any comments or ideas you may have.


Q: What if I have trouble using the website or the AI lesson plan generator?

A: Please contact us through the Web Form and we will be happy to assist you.
